New Beginnings

Initial Program

The Initial Program is a warm initiative by Jesus Youth VJEC, conducted every year for the freshers of our college. It gently welcomes students into campus life with care, joy, and inspiration. Each session is delivered with creativity and meaning, helping newcomers feel at home while building friendships, values, and dreams for the journey ahead.

ARISE

Join us this Sunday, 21 September 2025, at 9:00 AM in Vallopally Hall for Arise 2025. A joyful program by Jesus Youth VJEC, created for our freshers to celebrate new beginnings with faith, friendship, and inspiration.

Growing Together

College life is more than academics it’s about fellowship, values, and shared experiences. Arise is a step into that journey, helping you feel at home while inspiring you to shine brighter. At Arise, every smile, every moment, and every connection is a reminder that you’re never alone on this journey.
